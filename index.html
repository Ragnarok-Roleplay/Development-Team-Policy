Staff Rules

Please be reminded that any violation of the above rules will be dealt with accordingly. We want to ensure that we maintain a fair and enjoyable environment for the community and staff team.

  • Treat all members of the community with respect and professionalism.
  • Do not abuse your power as a staff member.
  • Follow all server rules and regulations.
  • Do not share confidential information about the community or its members.
  • Do not engage in any form of discrimination or harassment.
  • Do not use your staff position for personal gain.
  • Do not disclose any information about staff discussions or decisions to non-staff members.
  • Do not engage in any activity that would bring the community or staff team into disrepute.
  • Be willing to help other staff members and players whenever possible.
  • Be aware of the severity of the situation and act accordingly.

Bug Reports

  • Identify if the user is reporting an error or glitch.
  • Ask for a detailed description of the bug, steps to reproduce it, and any accompanying screenshots or videos.
  • Direct them to the Bug Reports section. If it's a more critical or complex bug, tag @Script Development.

(back to top)

Business Proposals

  • Understand if the user is proposing a new business idea or collaboration.
  • Ask for the core idea, potential benefits, and what they hope to achieve.
  • Either direct them to Business Enquiries ticket , or tag @Factions leader

(back to top)

Vehicle Related

  • Identify if the user has feedback, issues regarding a vehicle, or facing issues with vehicle location
  • Ask for the specific vehicle model, their feedback or issue, and any other relevant information, such as plate number.
  • Direct them to Vehicle Feedback or tag @Vehicle Development, unless it's for a change of garage location, then tag @FiveM Moderator

(back to top)

Clothing Related

  • Identify if the user has a suggestion or issue related to clothing.
  • Ask for the specific clothing item, their suggestion or issue, and any visuals if available.
  • Direct them to Clothing Suggestions or tag @Clothing Development.

(back to top)

Character Related

  • If it's for a name change, ensure they have been approved by DOJ, then tag @FiveM Admin
  • If they have recently changed PC or moved houses, their characters may have been wiped. Ask them to create a new character, with whatever name they want and once they've done so, tag @Owner @Management and @Lead Dev to solve this issue
